nsk.ai

Welcome to nsk.ai, the first ever non-profit AI society in the University Of Nigeria Nsukka. We are a community of curious and active learners who are passionate about AI, ML and DL.

About nsk.ai

As a university based community, nsk.ai is situated within the University Of Nigeria Nsukka. nsk.ai was created to help the members within the community to learn, share experiences and use their knowledge to create AI-enabled social impact solutions along with spreading AI awareness to the larger community. It is amied at creating a conducive atmosphere to help members engage each other and make it possible for us to share news, events, workshops and publications all in one place.

Learn

At nsk.ai, we believe in creating a supportive environment to facilitate learning and collaboration. Members within nsk.ai make it easier for beginners to get started in their AI journey through mentorships, one-on-one sessions, and collaborative projects.

Resources

nsk.ai has all the AI and machine learning resources you need to get started in your journey today! As a member of nsk.ai, you'll get access to a community of AI developers to help you along the way.

Community

Learn, grow and connect with the nsk.ai community that is focused on sharing learning experiences, building a skillset in AI, furthering goals and bringing together like-minded people to innovate and advance.
